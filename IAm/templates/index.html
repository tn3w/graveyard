Multiple Display Modes

Choose the integration style that fits your use case.

Inline Mode

Show the challenge directly without the checkbox. Perfect for registration flows.

<div class="iam-captcha"
     data-sitekey="YOUR_KEY"
     data-mode="inline"></div>

Bond to Button

Attach captcha to any button. Great for chat widgets or action buttons.

<button id="chat-btn">Start Chat</button>
<div class="iam-captcha"
     data-sitekey="YOUR_KEY"
     data-bond="#chat-btn"
     data-passive-mode="true"></div>

Auto-Position Float

Challenge automatically positions itself to stay within the viewport.

<div class="iam-captcha"
     data-sitekey="YOUR_KEY"
     data-float="auto"></div>
// Options: auto, top, bottom, left, right
